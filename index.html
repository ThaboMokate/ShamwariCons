About Us

Established 2018


Shamwari, is a Shona name meaning friends, is a close corporation company that specialises in events of any kind and magnitude, but only in the most stylish and best way possible.

As the company name suggests, Shamwari as not just a Consultancy company but also a platform where friends meet to make Business, to widen and consolidate their networks of contacts.

At Shamwari ,we aim to help clients bring more customers to their own business by ensuring that whatever services we provide is of the highest standard possible, which can only reflect positively on the clients as well.

Our Niche

The business is based on the principle known as the three P’s-Professionalism, Passion and Prosperity. This simply means that whatever task, we apply the highest standards of professionalism possible, without passion there can be no prosperity because the will to prosper will not be there at all. That is why we insist on fusing business with friendship, for true friends would always push each other to strive towards progress and prosperity.

We look forward to many years of friendship and prosperity, simply because the business strategy resolves around the need to provide quality service to the various target customers.

This will be undertaken through the establishment of a professional team and the provision of quality, customer-design service, catering to the client’s needs.

Mission Statement

Shamwari Consultancy (SC) has been found to fill a niche that exists in the service market. The research has shown us that the most companies present their service as a once off hence not paying much attention as to whether the client will need their service again in future. As a company, the aim is to build an image and sustainable competition advantage over the long term. We will make sure that we show the clients how we can add value to their business and in the process add value. The success of the business will be directly influenced by the dedicated to continually improving the quality and service in every aspect of the operation.

Founder and Owner Bio



Website: nonhlanhlaonverwacht.com
Nonhlanhla has a career spanning over 20 years, working for some of the leading players in banking and broadcasting in South Africa and the rest of the African continent. During her career, she acquired immense business, strategy development, and stakeholder management and innovation skills. Most recently, she has been at the forefront of introducing corporate governance initiatives in the broadcasting industry, which have included the implementation of a contracts management forum and a legal compliance conference.

She has introduced the first Brotherhood and Sisterhood GBV Dialogue. She intends to host this dialogue annually and create awareness on the scourge of GBV. 

Her drive and passion for education has seen her simultaneously Completing Master’s in Business Administration and an LLM in International Commercial Law (UK).

Nonhlanhla also holds a myriad of other qualifications, including a certificate in motor mechanics and a qualification in Business Digital Strategy from Emeritus. Her passion for building a healthy society, creating opportunities for others and utilising her skills and networks to uplift others have always underpinned Nonhlanhla’ s personal and professional journeys. This has led her to use her platform to create awareness on social issues like the scourge gender-based violence. She is working on a few articles relating to GBV and the other social issues. Her biggest drive is to see emancipated societies, hence she is always advocating for the concept of a hand up. Nonhlanhla is working with like-minded women as part of a consortium.
